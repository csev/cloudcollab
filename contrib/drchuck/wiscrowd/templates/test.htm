Simple LTI Test Client

Click here to use the Simple LTI Client. It is a form with sample form-post values. You can either post the values to this server - or post them to your own server. Since the Simple LTI uses simple POST parameters - you can even save the HTML from this form and make your own test form on your computer.

Simple LTI Service EndPoint
